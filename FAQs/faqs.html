Frequently Asked Questions
1. How can a beginner with no or little coding experience, gain knowledge from this initiative ?

We aim to build a platform where there is something for everyone. You will be getting a quality mentor support for your respective projects. There are number of projects with which one can enhance their abilities and gain command on the technology one wish to work.

2. Are there any charges for joining the program ?

Registration for this program is completely free of cost. One can join and come on board to showcase their passion, contribute to projects and boost up their skills with Lets Upgrade.

3. What are the prerequisites for one to get selected ?

All you need is passion to learn and hardworking attitude to succeed.

4. How will I get to know if I got selected for the progam ?

All the selected candidates will be receiving a mail once they get selected.

5. When the result of the programme will be announced ?

The result will be out on the successful completion of the program. So stay connected and keep on learning.

6. How we will get in touch with the mentors and the admins?

Once the candidate gets selected, he will be getting introduced to the mentors of Lets Upgrade.

7. How we will receive the certificate?

Once the candidate submits the project, mentor would be reviewing it and as soon as they approve your project, the candidate will receive certificate in digital form.

8. What are the perks available for the participants ?

The top performers as well as the top projects will be receiving several goodies. Other benefits include LU coins which can be redeemed to register to various premium courses on the LetsUpgrade Community.
