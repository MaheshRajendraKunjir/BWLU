ABOUT LETSUPGRADE

LetsUpgrade is an open community centric education where we believe upskilling of a person happens in offline learning session with our disruptive project based learning approach, where a student is a part of a big community of co-upgraders and a trainer is there to help you solve the problems in the league of achieving excellence in disruptive technology careers of future.
At LetsUpgrade from the start we make you grow in teams and help you upgrade yourself with the industry developed pro- grams, one on one mentorship sessions, job assistance during and end of the program.

ABOUT BUILD WITH LETSUPGRADE

Build with LetsUpgrade is the 2 month-long Open Source program conducted by LetsUpgrade with an aim to help beginners get started with Open Source Development while encouraging diversity. Throughout the program, participants contribute to different projects under the guidance of experienced mentors. Top participants get exciting goodies and opportunities. It’s a program focused on introducing students to open source software development. The main mantra is to technically inspire all the beginners.

R E G I S T R A T I O N S

Participants

If you have basic git skills or are first-timers or even veteran developers, Build with LetsUpgrade would give you a taste of contributing in an open-source projects. Comeon, Register yourself as a participant for Build with LetsUpgrade.

Mentors

Mentors are experts on the Open Source project the team is working on. At the beginning of the program, they provide a roadmap for the project (if it’s their own project or project provided by LetsUpgrade), otherwise, the Mentor needs to give issues to the participants based on the structure of the project provided by Project Managers. They are available throughout the whole program to review pull requests, help with concepts, and generally support the team. So try being a mentor in Build with LetsUpgrade.

HOW DOES IT WORK ?


A P P L Y

Interested students propose a project to work on project.

C O D E

Accepted students spend the time coding with guidance from a mentor.

S H A R E

Submit your code for others to see !

Issues are declared by Mentors, Managers keep check of structures of the projects and participants will comment on issues and make pull requests for the issue (claiming of issues by the participants will be done by first come first serve basis) and then work on the particular project issues.
